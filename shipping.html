Shipping Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

This Shipping Policy outlines the delivery terms and conditions for orders placed with MODA IMPETO ("we", "our", "us").

1. Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to the following regions:

  • Japan (nationwide)
  • United States (excluding Hawaii and Alaska)

We do not ship to any other countries or regions at this time.

2. Shipping Method

All orders are shipped using EMS (Japan Post).

Shipping carriers and methods are subject to change without prior notice.

3. Estimated Delivery Time

The estimated delivery time is approximately 1 week – 1 month from the date of shipment.

Please note that delivery times are estimates only and may vary depending on destination, customs processing, and other factors beyond our control.

4. Shipping Fees

Shipping is free of charge for all orders.

5. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international shipments, customs duties, import taxes, or other fees imposed by the destination country may apply.

Such charges are the responsibility of the customer.

6. Shipping Delays

We are not responsible for shipping delays caused during the transportation process, including but not limited to:

  • Carrier delays
  • Customs clearance delays
  • Weather conditions
  • Other circumstances beyond our control

Once an order has been handed over to the shipping carrier, MODA IMPETO assumes no liability for delivery delays.

7. Lost or Damaged Packages

MODA IMPETO is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged packages once they are in the possession of the shipping carrier.

Customers are advised to contact the carrier directly for further assistance.
